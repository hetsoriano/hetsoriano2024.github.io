About The Company

BYE BYE, PISAY! is an online shop managed by three Philippine Science High School Students. Besides selling a variety of pshs campuses (yes), BYE BYE, PISAY! aims to sell materials often used by pshs scholars such as scientific calculators, g-tech pens, etc. Additionally, to feel that✨ Pisay vibe ✨again, we chose to add multiple snacks that could be found in the campus' canteen.
